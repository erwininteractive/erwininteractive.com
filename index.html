About Us

Built on 25+ years of shipping software

Erwin Interactive was founded on a simple belief: every problem has a solution, and finding it starts with truly understanding the challenge. Based in Wisconsin, we bring decades of experience across web development, engineering systems, and emerging technologies, with a particular passion for helping startups and small teams punch above their weight.

We've helped bootstrapped startups and small teams ship products, improve their engineering systems, and move faster. We've navigated the evolution from static pages to AI-powered platforms. And through it all, one thing has stayed constant: our commitment to solving real problems for real people.

Whether you need a complete web application, guidance on AI adoption, or someone to untangle a complex technical challenge, we're here to help. And if the project needs expertise we don't have, we'll bring in the right specialists from our network.
